I am a Research Assistant at the Health and Social Care Unit, School of Public Health and Preventive Medicine, Monash University, under the supervision of Professor Helen Skouteris . I am also a Fully Registered Kindergarten Teacher, Educational Leader, Mentor with 3 years of teaching experiences across different types of early childhood education and care settings in Australia. I have a Master of Teaching (Early Childhood) degree from the University of Melbourne, a Bachelor’s Degree in Sociology from Nanjing University, and exchange experience in Developmental Psychology Department from Fudan University.

I am broadly interested in many areas of early childhood education. Currently, my main research interests are:

  • Centre-based social-emotional learning intervention for children with trauma experience.
  • Quality of early childhood education.
  • Child-centred participatory research.
